🍂 September already. Can you believe it?

📩 I'm mixing up the format for this newsletter. It's all part of a larger effort which I talk about on my Now Page to reduce friction in my workflow and better connect my tools with my creative motivation. So instead of thinking of my newsletter as a platform for essay writing (that really should be reserved for my blog), it's more about "what I'm up to right now and how might I inspire you today." Hope it sticks! 🤞 (also 😃 way 😁 more 🤩 emojis 😍 !)

🎙 I recently put out a new Fresh Fusion Podcast episode all about creative tools and workflow…not to get all in the weeds with nerdy productivity talk, but to encourage you that you can really go from meh to yay! if you take the time to experiment with the right approach. Sometimes we procrastinate and don't achieve the success we imagine for ourselves not because we don't want to create, but because the effort feels overwhelming. So it's vital to disover ways to reduce the overwhelm, to streamline, to make it feel more like fun and less like work.

📺 I've also been vlogging regularly again (as I alluded to in the previous issue of Creator Class) which has been a huge barrel of fun. So far I've done a few stints in the Portland area and one up in Seattle (next one coming hopefully within the next week), and while editing continues to be a major time sink (so much more work than writing or podcasting!), I feel like the emotional reward is well worth it. Vlogging truly benefits my mental health like no other medium. Maybe it's due to the "selfie" nature of it…getting to rewatch yourself enjoying amazing locations or trying exciting new activities (like kayaking on the Willamette River)!
